We had to put him down as he couldn't live long with his injuries. And our second dog we had for 5 years. This was until I started going to middle and pleaded with my parents to let me get a dog and this dog was Rocky. We've had Rocky for a really long time now and hes going on 10 years old. Only until recently did we get the new additions of our two kitties named Lily and Lilo.
